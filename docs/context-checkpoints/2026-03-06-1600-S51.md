**Validation rules:**
- Error (skip row): missing first_name OR missing last_name OR (no phone AND no NI number)
- Warning (import with flag): unrecognised trade, unparseable phone/date, unrecognised CSCS colour/grade
- Warning: no phone AND no email (uncontactable)
- Warning: no phone but has email (email-only contact)
- Duplicate detection: by phone (E.164 normalised), then by NI number
